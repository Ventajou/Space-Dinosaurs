Compatibility

The game has been tested with the following web browsers:

  • Internet Explorer 9
  • Firefox 4
  • Chrome 10

Controls

Racing levels

  • Up - Accelerate
  • Down - Brake
  • Left - Steer Left
  • Right - Steer Right

Shooter levels

  • Up - Move Up
  • Down - Move Down
  • Left - Move Right
  • Right - Move Left
  • Space - Fire

Cutscenes

Any key other than the ones listed above will display the text faster.
